Undergraduate Program: Information Systems

The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems program prepares students for all potential career positions in the IS field, including Systems Analysis and Design, Computer Networking, and Health Care Informatics.

Curriculum

  • IS 300 – Management Information Systems
  • IS 310 – Software and Hardware Concepts
  • IS 410 – Introduction to Database Design

For a detailed list of courses, visit the official curriculum page.

Admission Requirements

Prospective students must meet the general education requirements as explained in the UMBC Undergraduate Catalog. Specific course prerequisites include:

  • MATH 155 – Applied Calculus
  • IS 147 – Introduction to Computer Programming

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers in:

  • Systems Analysis and Design
  • Computer Networking
  • Health Care Informatics
